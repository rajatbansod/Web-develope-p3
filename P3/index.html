OUR STORY

Artefact Projects was founded in 1987 with a focus on delivering quality infrastructure services, including engineering and project management. The company has evolved into a leading consultancy firm, managing large-scale projects in transportation, airports, and urban development.

ABOUT US

Artefact Projects is an infrastructure consultancy company specializing in engineering, project management, and construction services. Established in 1987, it focuses on surface transportation, airport terminals, highways, and railway sectors. With a strong commitment to quality, they provide cost-effective and innovative solutions for large-scale infrastructure projects across India.
