  Airports




Artefact Projects with its enviable foreign collaboration has provided services for Airport development.


12th February, 2011 Honb’le Prime Minister of India Dr. Manmohan Singh inaugurated the new Trivandrum International Airport.  Artefact in collaboration with Rajiv and Moni Choudhary  Architects , WS Atkins , AJM – Planning and Urban Design Group Sdn. Bhd., Malaysia had successfully  provided for the Airport Concept, Design, Engineering, Project  Management and Consultancy Services since its inception in 2005, upto commissioning. The services included concept designs , architectural planning, interior design, structural designs , HAVC, mechanical and electrical facilities , security systems , communications , STP, ETP, electric sub-station, approach bridge and roadways , landscaping, environment management, cost and quality control and project supervision and management.


Complementing the Terminal Honb’le Prime Minister remarked:


“I compliment the Airports Authority of India for the commitment with which they have been building such impressive infrastructure in our airports.”


The Airport, built with most modern global standards, design, construction and practices, is at par with the best Airports in the world. With construction cost of Rs. 289 crores, it is a tubular three-storied fully air-conditioned building with a floor area of 20,000 sq meters, 30 check-in counters , 18 immigration counters , 18 Customs counters , 3 security checking units and 4 conveyor belts , a Common Users Terminal Equipment (CUTE), provision of Wi-Fi connectivity. The terminal can handle 1600 passengers, with 8 aircraft parking bays, 8 aerobridges, 600 cars parking, the ability to handle two A-300s and one Boeing 747 aircraft at a time.


The Airport is considered one of the best in the country and third best in structural design sand at par with some of the world’s most prestigious airports. Artefact acknowledging the valuable contribution of its foreign collaborators, considers a proper team work and highly professional focus for the project to be able to deliver the best professional and international skills required for the airport.


Trivandrum International Airport has direct flights to cities like Kuwait, Dubai, Kuala Lumpur, Singapore, Male, Colombo, Sharjah, Muscat, Bahrain, Doha, Jeddah and Abu Dhabi.
