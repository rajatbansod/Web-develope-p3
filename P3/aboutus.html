  Chairman's Message


 

ARTEFACT PROJECTS LTD.., inspired by ‘Vishwakarma’, the Engineering deity revered in India, undertook to establish itself in the field of consultancy for infrastructure and specialized project services since late go.

With combination of technical and Project Management experience, we have been able to bring projects from concepts and designs stage to their successful operations successfully.


These Projects reflect our commitment, sincerity, dedication and sense of purpose, apparent in all our ventures that result in client satisfaction.


The company is providing project services for Highways, Urban Infrastructure, Airports, Mining Services & Railways.

 

 
“Every Achievement is a milestone, not a destination. With every success, goals keep getting bigger”
 


  History


The Company has built its reputation as consultant for National Highways.



1987

Artefact Projects Ltd. was incorporated in Nagpur 1987, strongly committed to serving India's development through their engineering capabilities.

1998

In 1998 ventured into Infrastructure Consultancy Services as Engineers, Planners & Project Management Consultants.

2006

SECTORIAL Focus on Services for Highways, Airports, Urban Development (include SEZs, Reality, Townships). Gross Fees Receipts in past 5 years (FY-2006 to FY-2010) Rs. 113.64 Mns.

2010

Completed Successful Private Placement exercise of 9,00,000 Eq Shares and 10,22,400 warrants of Rs.10/- each at a premium of Rs.91/- each, amounting to Rs.194.2 Mns.

2012

Net Worth in the FY 2009-10: Rs. 276.99 Mns. National & International Operations.

2019

Highway assignments Ongoing involve; 13 Construction Projects with 732.725 Km length, Rs. 7732.85 Cr. Billion Project Cost and 04 O&M Projects with 586.092 Km Length and Rs. 1200 Cr Billion Project Cost. Gross contracted fees as on sept 2019 stood at Rs.131.58 Cr and balance amount to be billed in next 3 years exceeds Rs. 25.60 Cr.
