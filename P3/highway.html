HIGHWAYs

Home - Project

  Highways





Highlights


  • * Detailed Design & DPR – 681.50 Kms.
  • * Construction Supervision – 1157.48 Kms.
  • * Supervision of O&M Contracts - 1418.659 Kms.
 

Artefact has rendered specialized Project Services for Road Sector for more than 10000 Kms. Services include :-

I. Detailed Design / DPR
 + 

Artefact has completed detailed design & DPR for more than 681.50 kms of highways & other Roads. DPR prepared by Artefact were held good for construction & were implemented without modifications.

II. As Independent Engineers for PPP Project (Design Review, Implementation, Monitoring & Project Management) services :-
 + 

Artefact was one of the first few Companies to act as Independent Engineers for PPP Highways Projects. The scope of works included expert services in Design, Construction Financial Closure, O&M Supervision and Quality Control of Highway ongoing assignment are for 17 major National Highway Projects for 4/6 laning of 1735 Kms of NH sections in various States Pan India.

III. Project Management
 + 

Artefact, along with Meinhardt Pte, Singapore, has undertaken services as Project Management Consultants for prestigious Chhattisgarh Rural Roads Development project funded by ADB, for Design, Supervision & Construction of 2100 Kms Roads in 6 Districts of Chhattisgarh State with Project Construction cost of Rs.6000 Millions approx.

IV. For O & M Supervision
 + 

Artefact has rendered Comprehensive Construction and Operation and Maintenance (O&M) of prestigious Tolled National Highways and State Highways for length exceeding 550 Kms. Salient features of the above O&M Contracts include Service level based contract - Use of Advanced Highways Traffic Management Systems (HTMS), Computerized Automated Toll Systems, various Advanced Techniques in Highway and Public Safety, Public Relations and Law Enforcement, Road Property and Facility Management.

V. As Lender’s Engineer
 + 

Artefact has also rendered services as Lender’s Engineer to various Financial Institutions viz., State Bank of India, Infrastructure Leasing and Finance Services (IL&FS) and SICOM for the development of Highway Projects.

VI. Design & Supervision – Urban Roads
 + 

Artefact has rendered services for Urban Transport Infrastructure as Project Management Consultants for DPR, supervision of development schemes for the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) under Mumbai Urban Infrastructure Project (MUIP), and Traffic Dispersal Scheme for the development of Urban Road Infrastructure network in Mumbai. The major component of prestigious MUIP project in Mumbai involved development of 6/8 lane missing links, 4 lane ROB (600 mtrs.), 2 Flyovers (4 lane) of length 500 mtrs and 600 mtrs & Major Bridges. Similar assignment was also completed by Artefact for Agra City, which included preparation of Detailed Design of more than 40 Kms. Urban Road stretches within Agra City, under Integrated Road Development Program (IRDP).
Artefact has completed Consultancy assignment for World Bank Funded Project of Pedestrian Grade Separators under MUIP for the construction of 6 subways in Mumbai (Client MCGM). Artefact is now stepping up its bidding, Consultancy assignments to target a jump of 100% growth for Revenue and Projects Assignments in coming years. Artefact also seized the opportunity to provide Services to the opening up of Railways and 100 Smart City Urban Infra Development Plans.

Artefact has under bidding, Consultancy assignments involving fees of more than Rs. 900 Million, a sizeable growth fees Revenue and numerous Projects Assignments are expected in coming years, in road sector.
