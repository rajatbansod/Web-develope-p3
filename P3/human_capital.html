THE PROPELLING POWERHOUSE


ARTEFACT is a “Knowledge Centric” Organization. The Company values its human capital as biggest Asset. The Company has a sizable strength of Technical, Contract, Legal and Project Management Professionals. The Engineering staff strength exceeding 300 numbers is ever increasing. They have National and International experience and a proven track-record.  The broad break-up of manpower strength is as below:




ARTEFACT is spearheaded by enterprising young professionals who have stood by the evolution and grew the Company till its present position. These promoters are, all Engineers & Chartered Accountants. With their Engineering & Project Management qualifications and skills in place, they combined their professional experience and expertise to guide the Company for growth along professional lines.


Key Technical Professionals include Civil Engineers, Structural Engineers, Environmental Experts, Horticulturists, Electrical Engineers, IT Experts, HTMS & Toll Specialists, Traffic Economist, Safety Experts, CAD Engineers, Surveyors, Quantity Surveyors, Highway and Bridge Designers, Geologists, Contract Engineers, other Professional staff includes Legal Experts, Financial and BOT/PPP Experts, Architects. Urban Planners, Material and Plant Experts, Institutional Experts.


The Company has been able to collate a resource pool of experienced professionals and experts from India and overseas through its foreign collaboration also besides its own team.

  • Participative, flexible with an eye for detail
  • Ability to nurture, develop and retain talent
  • Strive for excellence through improvisation
  • Focused on Enterprise Governance
  • Committed to “Customer Centricity”
